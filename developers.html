Every language in the world, for everyone.

Wikitongues is an open source effort to create a video archive of native speakers and signers of the all the world's languages. The project is a vehicle for debate around the value and legitimacy of culture as expressed through language. We maintain ambassadors responsible for recording speakers and signers in their respective regions and conducting missions to produce content in areas of difficult access.

Our vision is to create a living database of video interviews with multiple speakers of all the world's languages, hosted on an open source platform that allows for frequent and new user-generated submissions and translations, facilitating not only a popular conversation about the value of identity but the research of linguists, sociologists and other academics in the social sciences.
